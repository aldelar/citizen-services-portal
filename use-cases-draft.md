### Current Processes for Citizen Services in Los Angeles

Citizens in Los Angeles increasingly demand seamless, digital-first interactions with government services, yet many processes remain bogged down by legacy systems that involve manual reviews, fragmented coordination, and limited online options. This leads to inefficiencies, such as prolonged wait times and redundant paperwork, ultimately straining resources and diminishing public trust. By infusing AI-driven virtual assistants into these workflows, agencies could streamline routine inquiries, guide users through applications, and provide real-time status updates, fostering more responsive and transparent experiences.

### Iterative Selection Approach

This summary highlights how outdated, fragmented systems contribute to erosion of trust through delays and inconsistencies. For the next iteration, proposed top 3 for prototyping:

1. **Building Permits & Renovations**  
2. **Business License Applications**  
3. **Utility Connection & Disconnection**

By deploying AI-powered citizen service portals focused on these high-impact areas, Los Angeles can deliver consistent, accessible, and responsive experiences—reducing administrative overhead while building stronger citizen trust and a more digitally mature public sector.
