  • Alyssa Leiva
  • November 02, 2022

L.A. Sanitation S.A.F.E Collection Center Operations at UCLA

LA Sanitation (LASAN) has established programs for Los Angeles City and L.A. County residents to safely dispose of their household hazardous waste (HHW) and electronic waste (E-waste). These types of waste can be dropped off at collection sites known as S.A.F.E. Centers or mobile collection events located throughout the City of Los Angeles. LA City operates a collection center on UCLA campus.

 UCLA does not manage this program and is unable to provide information about this program.

For assistance:  1-800-773-2489

 L.A. City S.A.F.E. Website    

NOTE: S.A.F.E Program will be CLOSED temporarily beginning May 8, 2025.  
PLEASE NOTE: Any waste left outside the facility is considered illegal dumping and is subject to fines.
