About ePlanLA

The City of Los Angeles’ Department of Building and Safety (LADBS) now offers customers the ability to submit project plans, drawings, and other construction project documents electronically through our recently launched online service, ePlanLA.

ePlanLA - short for Electronic Plan Review Los Angeles - is a convenient, secure, and efficient way to apply for permits online.

ePlanLA currently has the following types of permits available to apply for online:

  • → Building Permit: Building, Soft-Story Retrofit, Tenant Improvement, Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU), Affordable Housing, Fences and Blockwalls, Swimming Pool and Spa, Non-Building, Demolition, Relocation, Sign, Use of Land, Grading, Grading Report
  • → Mechanical Permit: Fire Sprinkler, Plumbing, HVAC, Elevator
  • → Electrical Permit: Electrical, Solar Photovoltaic
  • → Preinspection: Demolition, Grading, Signs
