Type 1, 2, and 3 Interconnection

*All system size ratings submitted as kW AC (battery) and kW AC-CEC (solar).
Type 1, 2, 3 and 4 Interconnection Process.

    Type 1

    Type 1 – PV (NEM)/BESS

    Customers or contractors can apply under the Type 1 Interconnection Process for PV (NEM) and/or BESS projects that meet the following Service Planning requirements.

    Type 1 Interconnection Process should be followed if the proposed system is up to and including 30kW* aggregate system size* and does not include:

    1) a service panel upgrade;

    2) a three (3) phase service connected to the customer’s project;

    3) projects that contain any non-NEM eligible cogeneration components; and

    4) other multi-meter interconnection types such as apartment buildings, tract home projects, etc. (e.g. multi-meter interconnections are not “single meter” interconnection applications).

    Type 1 Interconnection Process will typically involve these steps:

    *Note: Additional LADWP solar programs information is available on the Solar Programs page.

    Step 1
    • Apply - LADWP Online Interconnection application:
      • Upload required documentation
      • Upload required photos
    • Apply - LADBS permit application (PV and BESS, if applicable)
    Step 2
    • Receive confirmation email and Interconnection or NEM Work Request (WR) Number
    • Pay Expenditure Recovery Charge(s)
    Step 3
    • LADWP ESR Staff preliminary review / feedback:
      • ESR Pre–Construction Mtg/"OK to build" – Approval to begin installation
    • Obtain approvals:
      • Small System Interconnection Agreement (IA) review, if applicable
      • ESR final inspection and approval
      • LADBS final permit inspection and approval
    Step 4
    • Complete LADWP construction scheduling, installation, and testing and commissioning.
    • Complete meter scheduling and installation (field order)
    Step 5
    • Obtain LADWP NEM Permission to Operate (PTO) Notice
    Type 2

    Type 2 – PV (NEM)/BESS/COGEN

    Customers or contractors can apply under the Type-2 Interconnection Process for PV (NEM) and/or BESS projects that meet the following Service Planning requirements.

    Type 2 Interconnection Process should be followed if the proposed system is up to and including 30kW* aggregate system size* (or 10kW and below, 3 phase) and does include:

    1) a service panel upgrade; and/or

    2) other small multi-meter interconnection types such as apartment buildings, tract home projects, etc. i.e. not a single meter interconnection application and/or

    3) projects that contain any non-NEM eligible cogeneration components up to and including 30kW* aggregate system size.

    Type 2 Interconnection Process will typically involve these steps:
     

    *Note: Additional LADWP solar programs information is available online at www.ladwp.com/solar.

    Step 1
    • Apply - LADWP Online Interconnection application:
      • Upload required documentation
      • Upload required photos
    • Apply - LADBS permit application (PV, BESS, or Cogeneration as applicable)
    Step 2
    • Receive confirmation email and Interconnection or Cogeneration Work Request (WR) Number
    • Pay Expenditure Recovery Charge(s)
    Step 3
    • LADWP ESR Staff preliminary review / feedback:
      ESR Pre–Construction Mtg/"OK to build" – Approval to begin installation
    • Obtain approvals:
      • Engineer review and approval, if applicable
      • ESR final inspection and approval
      • Small System Interconnection Agreement (IA) review, if applicable
      • LADBS final permit inspection and approval
    Step 4
    • Complete LADWP construction scheduling, installation, and testing and commissioning.
    • Complete meter scheduling and installation (field order)
    Step 5
    • Obtain LADWP NEM Permission to Operate (PTO) Notice
    Type 3

    Type 3 – PV (NEM)/BESS/COGEN

    Customers or contractors can apply for the Type 3 Interconnection Process for PV (NEM), BESS and/or Cogeneration projects that meet the following Service Planning requirements. All system size ratings submitted as kW AC and kW AC-CEC (solar).

    Type 3 Interconnection Process should be followed if the proposed system installation is greater than 30kW* and less than 1MW (for NEM and BESS) aggregate system size, for all non-NEM eligible cogeneration project greater than 30kW* and less than 1MW aggregate system size, and/or does include:

    1) a part of a new tract home project (i.e. five or more homes being interconnected);

    2) a three (3) phase service connected to the customer’s project with aggregate system size* greater than 10kW*; and

    3) other large, multi-meter interconnection types such as apartment buildings, tract home projects, etc. i.e. not a single meter interconnection application.

    Type 3 Interconnection Process will typically involve these steps:  

    *Note: Additional LADWP solar programs information is available online at www.ladwp.com/solar.

    Step 1
    • Apply - LADWP Online Interconnection application:
      • Upload required documentation
      • Upload required photos
    • Apply - LADBS permit application (PV, BESS, or Cogeneration as applicable)
    Step 2
    • Receive confirmation email and Interconnection or Cogeneration Work Request (WR) Number
    • Pay Expenditure Recovery Charge(s)
    Step 3
    • LADWP PV/BESS Staff preliminary review / feedback:
      • Engineer review
      • ESR Pre–Construction Meeting
      • ESR "OK to build" Approval to begin installation
    • Obtain approvals:
      • Engineer approval
      • ESR final inspection and approval
      • Standard Offer Interconnection Agreement (IA) approval
      • LADBS final permit inspection and approval
    Step 4
    • Complete LADWP construction scheduling, installation, and testing and commissioning.
    • Complete meter scheduling and installation (field order)
    Step 5
    • Obtain LADWP NEM Permission to Operate (PTO) Notice
    Type 4

    Type 4 – COGEN ONLY

    Customers or contractors can apply for the Type 4 Interconnection Process for PV, BESS and/or Cogeneration project that meet the following Service Planning requirements. All system size ratings submitted as kW AC and kW AC-CEC (solar), as applicable.

    Type 4 Interconnection Process should be followed If the proposed installation is greater than 1MW (for PV and BESS) aggregate system size*, for all non-NEM eligible cogeneration project greater than 1MW aggregate system size.

    *Sum total of all forms of customer owned generation on-site. Example: For inverter-based generation, the sum total of (if applicable) all non-solar dedicated inverter nameplate ratings (kW AC), or kW AC-CEC (solar) values.

    Type 4 Interconnection Process will typically involve these steps:

    *Note: Additional LADWP solar programs information is available online at www.ladwp.com/solar.

    Step 1
    • Apply - LADWP Online Interconnection application:
      • Upload required documentation
    • Apply - LADBS permit application (PV, BESS, or Cogeneration as applicable)
    Step 2
    • Receive confirmation email and Cogeneration Work Request (WR) Number
    • Pay Expenditure Recovery Charge(s)
    Step 3
    • LADWP PV/BESS Staff preliminary review / feedback:
      • Engineer review
      • ESR Pre–Construction Meeting
      • ESR "OK to build" Approval to begin installation
    • Obtain approvals:
      • Engineer approval
      • ESR final inspection and approval
      • Standard Offer Interconnection Agreement (IA) approval
      • LADBS final permit inspection and approval
    Step 4
    • Complete LADWP construction scheduling, installation, and testing and commissioning.
    • Complete meter scheduling and installation (field order)
    Step 5
    • Obtain LADWP NEM Permission to Operate (PTO) Notice
